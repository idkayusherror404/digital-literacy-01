## About This Project

This repository is my Digital Literacy Portfolio, built as part of the CSE0001 course at VIT Bhopal. The assignment asked us to take on the role of a Student Digital Ambassador — someone who helps their peers navigate the digital world safely and professionally. The portfolio covers five tasks across five modules, from creating an infographic to studying cybercrime.

Everything here — the write-ups, designs, emails, and checklists — is my own work.

## Module Summaries

**Task 1 — Digital Literacy Infographic**  
Created a one-page visual infographic covering five topics: what digital literacy is, useful student tools, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette. Tool used: HTML and CSS.

**Task 2 — Student Digital Portfolio**  
Set up profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. Each platform serves a different purpose — GitHub for code, LinkedIn for professional networking, and Kaggle for data science practice.

**Task 3 — Coding and Collaboration Platforms**  
Completed a beginner challenge on HackerRank and built a 5-question Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms.

**Google Form Quiz Link:** https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSf5mfl_SydkPI9rMcjuaWycBC2pPP2Be-n10-E3nzEi6g3NNA/viewform?usp=publish-editor

**Task 4 — Email Etiquette**  
Drafted two professional emails — one requesting a deadline extension from a professor, and one expressing internship interest to a company. Also created a social media Do's and Don'ts checklist.

**Task 5 — Cybercrime Awareness**  
Researched UPI/Online Payment Fraud as my chosen cybercrime type. Wrote a 200-word case study and a prevention checklist with 8 actionable tips specific to college students in India.
